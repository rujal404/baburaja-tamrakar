Custom Metal Emblems for Nepal Police & Tactical Training College

This handcrafted metal emblem project represents the Nepal Police and the specialized Nepal Police Tactical Training College, showcasing a perfect blend of authority, tradition, and skilled Nepali craftsmanship. Our expertise in bespoke metalwork ensures every piece is uniquely crafted to meet organizational standards while preserving the cultural essence of Nepalese artistry.

Custom Institutional Metal Logo for SAARC

This custom crafted metal emblem for the South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC) showcases exceptional Nepali craftsmanship, blending traditional techniques with modern precision. Designed using high-quality brass, the emblem features intricate detailing, a polished finish, and long-lasting durability—making it ideal for official institutions, conference halls, and organizational branding. Each piece is carefully handcrafted to reflect SAARC’s identity and regional unity, ensuring a premium and professional appearance. Our expertise in bespoke metal logo design guarantees unique, high-quality results for government bodies, international organizations, and corporate clients seeking timeless and impactful visual branding.

Inspired by the Vision of Pramila Giri

This bespoke art installation features a handcrafted brass and copper sculptural relief, meticulously cast to capture the fluid, organic strength characteristic of contemporary Nepalese metalwork. Produced by Babu Metal Crafts, the piece serves as a focal point within a larger, vibrant mural that draws direct inspiration from the spiritual and abstract forms of renowned artist Pramila Giri. By blending traditional casting techniques with bold, modern aesthetics, this project honors the heritage of Himalayan art while pushing the boundaries of architectural decor.
